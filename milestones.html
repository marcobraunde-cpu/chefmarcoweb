Achievements

Milestones

Key recognitions, achievements, and defining moments across a 20-year career in luxury hospitality.

◆

Best Restaurant & Bar — Jakarta 2009

Winner of the prestigious Best Restaurant & Bar Jakarta award for Times Square Resto & Bar during his tenure at Kempinski Hotel Indonesia. A landmark achievement in his early career in Asia.

◆

Salon Culinaire Gold Medal

As Team Coach for Grand Hyatt Dubai, led the culinary team to a Gold Medal at the Salon Culinaire competition after three years of dedicated coaching and preparation.

◆

Judge — Salon Culinaire Asia

Appointed as culinary judge at Salon Culinaire Asia from 2018 to 2020, evaluating top culinary talent across the region and contributing to the elevation of professional cooking standards in Southeast Asia.

◆

World Economic Forum — Davos 2022

Member of the exclusive Gastronomy Team for the World Economic Forum in Davos, Switzerland. Served as Hygiene & Work-Law Team Leader during one of the world's most scrutinised high-profile events.

◆

ISO 22000 Certification

Spearheaded the upgrade of the Food Safety Management System from HACCP to ISO 22000 at Grand Hyatt Bali — setting a new benchmark for food safety standards across the resort's five restaurants and banquet operations.

◆

ASEAN Economic Meeting — AEM55

Directed the full culinary operation for the ASEAN Economic Meeting (AEM55) at Padma Hotel, Central Java — delivering seamless dining service for up to 6,000 guests per day at one of Southeast Asia's most important diplomatic gatherings.

◆

15,000-Cover Events

Delivered banquets and MICE events for up to 15,000 covers at Jakarta Convention Center — among the largest culinary operations ever executed in Indonesia, demonstrating unmatched large-scale event mastery.

◆

Outside Catering Brand Creation

Conceived and launched a standalone Outside Catering Brand at Jakarta Convention Center, generating a new revenue stream and positioning the venue competitively in the premium catering market.

◆

Member — Gastronomy-Swiss

Recognised member of Gastronomy-Swiss since 2021, affirming his standing within the elite circle of Swiss hospitality and culinary professionals.

◆

Hyatt Place Dubai — Pre-Opening

Core team member for the pre-opening of Hyatt Place Dubai while serving as Executive Sous Chef at Grand Hyatt Dubai — contributing to the successful launch of a new brand in the competitive UAE market.
